Overview

Basic Information

  • Name: Son Goku (Monkey King)
  • Race: Saiyan
  • Birthplace: Mount Paozu
  • Age: Over 40 (Timeline dependent)

Character Description

Son Goku is the main protagonist of the Dragon Ball franchise. Born as a Saiyan and sent to Earth as an infant, Goku was found and raised by Grandpa Gohan. Despite his extraterrestrial origins, he became Earth's greatest defender and the strongest martial artist in the world.

Personality

  • Simple-minded but pure-hearted
  • Loves fighting and training
  • Dedicated to protecting Earth
  • Values friendship and camaraderie
  • Never gives up, no matter the odds
